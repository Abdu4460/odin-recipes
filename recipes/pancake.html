Here's the recipe for morning-after pancakes

Description

Another night, another disappointment. But, the morning doesn't have to be any more disappointing! Introducing: "The bar has never been lower to the point where making this simple breakfast dish counts as a genuine form of compassion" pancakes! (nvm the cynicism, i'm in that kind of mood today, these pancakes slap).

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Competence

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the egg and the sugar.
  2. Pour in the milk then mix until homogeneous.
  3. Sift all the dry stuff in then mix until the batter forms.
  4. Pour in the oil, mix again until homogeneous.
  5. On a heated pan, pour in some of the batter.
  6. When the surface of the pancake becomes bubbly, flip it till it cooks from the other side.
  7. Repeat until the batter is finished.
  8. Serve with butter, maple syrup, chocolate syrup, or anything that fits and that you enjoy.
